## 📌 Section Name: "Careers at SkillBuddy"

**Why "Careers"?**
The existing "Jobs" page already shows jobs posted BY clients for service providers.
This new "Careers" page is completely different — it shows SkillBuddy's own internal job openings where SkillBuddy is HIRING people to work FOR the company (developers, designers, marketers, support staff, etc.)

**Add "Careers" to the navbar** between "Jobs" and "FAQs":
```
Home | Services | Jobs | Careers | FAQs | Contact Us
```

**Route:** `/careers`
