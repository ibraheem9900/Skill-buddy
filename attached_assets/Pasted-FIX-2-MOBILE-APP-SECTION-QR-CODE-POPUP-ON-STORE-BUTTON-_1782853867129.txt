FIX 2 — MOBILE APP SECTION: QR CODE POPUP ON STORE BUTTON CLICK
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PROBLEM: Clicking Play Store or App Store logo/button should show a QR code popup modal, not redirect to external link.

FIX — KEEP STORE BUTTONS AS IS (do not remove them).
ADD a QR code popup modal that appears when either button is clicked.

POPUP MODAL BEHAVIOR:
- When user clicks Google Play button OR App Store button: open a modal/popup
- Modal appears centered on screen with a dark overlay behind it
- Clicking outside the modal OR clicking X button closes it
- Press Escape key also closes it
- Separate QR codes for each store:
  Google Play click → show Google Play QR
  App Store click → show App Store QR
- Use these QR image URLs:
  Google Play QR: https://api.qrserver.com/v1/create-qr-code/?size=180x180&data=https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.skillbuddy
  App Store QR:   https://api.qrserver.com/v1/create-qr-code/?size=180x180&data=https://apps.apple.com/app/skillbuddy

MODAL DESIGN:
- Background overlay: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.6), covers full screen, z-index 9999
- Modal box:
  background: white (light theme) / #1e1e1e (dark theme)
  border-radius: 20px
  padding: 32px
  width: 300px
  centered on screen (position fixed, top 50%, left 50%, transform -50% -50%)
  box-shadow: 0 20px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.3)
- X close button: top-right corner of modal, 32x32px, circular, muted color
- Modal content (top to bottom):
  1. Store logo icon (Google Play or App Store — small, 32px)
  2. Heading: "Scan to Download" — font-bold, text-lg, color foreground
  3. Subtext: "Point your phone camera at the QR code" — text-sm, text-muted-foreground
  4. QR code image: 180x180px, centered, border-radius 8px, white bg padding 8px
  5. Bottom text: "SkillBuddy — Available on [store name]" — text-xs, muted

MODAL ANIMATION (use Framer Motion):
- Entrance: scale 0.8 → 1, opacity 0 → 1, duration 250ms, ease easeOut
- Exit: scale 1 → 0.8, opacity 1 → 0, duration 200ms
- Overlay: opacity 0 → 0.6 on open, 0.6 → 0 on close
- Use AnimatePresence to handle mount/unmount
